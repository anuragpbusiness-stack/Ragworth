## 3. Operational Excellence (The "Deep Research" Edge)
To maintain prestige, Ragon Co must operate with 100% reliability. We will implement the "Agency Gold Standard":

*   **The Golden 90 Days:** Set strict 30/60/90-day milestones for every client. Deliver a "Quick Win" within the first 14 days to lock in trust.
*   **Value-Based Pricing:** We don't charge for hours. We charge for **ROI**. Our fee is typically 10-20% of the value/savings we create.
*   **Quarterly Business Reviews (QBR):** Every 90 days, we conduct a high-level strategic review with the client's C-suite to show data-driven results and upsell the next phase.
*   **The "Human Director" Model:** We use AI to do 80% of the work, but we sell the 20% "Human Strategy" that ensures the AI doesn't hallucinate or deviate from brand goals.
