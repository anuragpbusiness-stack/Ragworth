# Ragworth: Institutional Brand Identity
**Vision:** Institutional Luxury | Restrained Power | Architectural Minimalism

## 1. Core Visual Principles
*   **The "Prestige" Anchor:** We do not chase trends. We embody "Old-Money Modernity."
*   **Restraint over Flash:** No "AI-bro" aesthetics, neon, or aggressive futurism. 
*   **Negative Space:** The brand must breathe. Emptiness is a signal of wealth and confidence.

## 2. Color Palette
| Element | Color Name | HEX Code | Psychological Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Core Background** | Deep Architectural Black | `#0B0B0D` | Cinematic charcoal, visual silence. |
| **Primary Typography**| Platinum Ivory | `#E3DED6` | Tailored, mature, avoids "cheap" pure white. |
| **Secondary Accent** | Warm Stone Metallic | `#A89E91` | Refinement without the gaudiness of gold. |
| **Structural Divider**| Soft Graphite Taupe | `#6F6961` | Creates rhythm and hierarchy. |

## 3. Typography & UI
*   **Main Wordmark:** Hybrid serif-modern. Sharpness meets softness.
*   **Spacing:** High tracking (letter spacing) and wide margins.
*   **Motion:** Extremely subtle fades. No "popping" or "glitch" effects.

## 4. Brand Atmosphere
*   **Keywords:** Private Equity, Architectural Penthouse, Tailored Suit, Matte Graphite.
*   **Tone of Voice:** "Quiet Infrastructure Power." High competence, low noise.

## 5. Usage Rules
*   **NEVER:** Use gradients, glows, shadows, or saturated colors.
*   **ALWAYS:** Maintain absolute minimalism. If in doubt, remove an element.
